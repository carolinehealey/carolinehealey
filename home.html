Get to know me!

Caroline Healey is a sophomore at Elon University. She is majoring in Strategic Communications and Media Analytics with a minor in Professional Sales. She is interested in public relations, media design, and all things fashion. Caroline hopes to one day live in Boston or New York and represent a brand in the creative field.

Caroline is a New England girl at heart, originally being from Bedford, New Hampshire. She loves snowy days and exploring cozy small towns. Caroline is a master skier and enjoys hitting the slopes in her free time with family and friends. She loves iced vanilla lattes, yoga and country music concerts.

After graduating highschool, Caroline took a gap year. She participating in two Pacific Discovery trips. Her gap year was the most impactful year of her life. For the first semester of the year, Caroline traveled the Western United States of America. She went to 9 states: Colorado, Utah, Idaho, Wyoming, Montanta, Washington, Oregon, Nevada, and California. In her second semester, she travelled around Thailand for 85 days. Caroline learned about Buddhism while traversing the scenic land. She worked with elephants in the north and relaxed on the beaches of the south. Caroline made life long connections with friends along the way.
